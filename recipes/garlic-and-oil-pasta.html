Ingredients

  • Kosher salt
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Pinch red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/2 lemon, zested, optional
  • Freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, optional
